About Me
Hey! My name is Giovanni and I am a Machine Learning engineer.
I was born in a small town in the middle of Sicily surrounded by nature and food. When I was a child a developed a passion for computers and some years ago I got my bachelor degree in computer engineering. Today I live in Stockholm where I am currently studying Machine learning at KTH. During my free time i like sleeping, singing, reading, playing chess, making pizza and much much more. Welcome to my life!
